The adaptive aI composer

Melodrive is an AI composer that sits inside a digital world. It listens to events happening in a game or XR experience and creates music that fits the mood. On the fly.

Released as a Unity plugin, Melodrive can create music for any interactive environment. Creators can create new songs instantly, choose styles and instruments, and have a seamless, emotionally-driven music experience.
